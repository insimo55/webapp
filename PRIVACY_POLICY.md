# Privacy Policy

This Telegram bot is a service tool for drilling and mud engineering teams.

## Data processing
The bot does NOT collect, store, or analyze personal user data.

## Message handling
The bot processes messages only inside predefined working group chats and topics.
Messages may be forwarded to an internal company dashboard for operational purposes.

## Personal chats
The bot does not initiate or maintain private conversations with users.

## Third-party services
The bot interacts only with an internal backend system.

## Contact
If you have any questions regarding this policy, contact the system administrator.
